A relaxing afternoon sipping tea

"I love high tea!"

My friend exclaims after hearing that I celebrated my 30th birthday with afternoon tea at The Palm Court. Thus kicking off our journey trying out different tea spots in New York. Slowly our pair grew into a small group that met every few months to celebrate a bit of decadence and to debate whether we were having afternoon tea or high tea. The highlight of afternoon tea differs for each Guest from the scones to sandwiches, making this list particularly fun to filter by Guest.
