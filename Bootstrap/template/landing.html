About Me

I love understanding customers and connecting them to products and services they will love.

An amateur sleuth at heart and marketing executive by profession, I gather insights on customers and solve the mystery of how companies can tap into growth by meeting their customers’ needs. Whether its chefs, super fans, or coders, I marry my experience growth marketing at start-ups with my consulting experience advising marketing leaders at enterprises to build and execute strategy.

I’ve led marketing teams to develop brand strategy, design and launch products, and optimize marketing channels to meet revenue targets. I have an MBA from the Yale School of Management and dual degrees from The University of Texas at Austin, BBA in Finance and BA in Economics. In my free time if I’m not watching a crime documentary, you’ll find me testing new recipes in the kitchen, throwing ceramics, or exploring the city.
