About Me

I love understanding customers and connecting them to products and services they will love.

I’m a Growth and Product Marketer. Most recently I led Product Marketing at Codecademy. I was also part of the Spring ’18 cohort of the Reforge Growth Series, an educational bootcamp for growth professionals. Prior to Codecademy, I spent five years consulting for Accenture, focusing on project management, business strategy, and data analysis. I have a MBA from the Yale School of Management and dual degrees from The University of Texas at Austin, BBA in Finance and BA in Economics.
