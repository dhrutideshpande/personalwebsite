Listicles Galore

"Who made this list?!"

I often wonder when reading a listicle reviewing food. How does their preference affect the rating? Am I actually getting the best list for me? Note “for me.” No two diners are alike and thus its unlikely that no two restaurants are going to be everyone’s favorite. Over the years I have read many listicles and found that none of them allow me to filter for someone who shares my preferences, giving birth to this site. The goal of the site is to help you find the next place to dine based of filtering for preferences. On each page take a look at the guest and find the guest or guests that resonate with you. Then filter the restaurants by that person to get a better list FOR YOU.
